Research Participation Consent

Your contribution to EV charging security research

Study Purpose
You are invited to participate in groundbreaking research to understand user decision-making and enhance the security of electric vehicle charging station interfaces. Your responses will help develop better security measures for EV charging infrastructure.
What You'll Do
  • Complete a brief demographic questionnaire
  • Engage with simulated EV charging scenarios
  • Make decisions about charging station security
  • Provide feedback on your experience
Duration: Approximately 30-45 minutes
Benefits & Risks
Benefits: Contribute to safer EV charging technology for everyone.
Risks: No foreseeable risks or discomforts associated with participation.
Privacy & Confidentiality
Your participation is completely confidential. All data will be anonymized and securely stored. Only the research team will access the data, and no personal information will be disclosed in publications.
Important Note
Your participation is entirely voluntary. You may withdraw at any time without any penalty or loss of benefits.
