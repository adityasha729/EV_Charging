Welcome to the EV Charging Simulation

Instructions for the game

You are an EV driver navigating a charging task.

Your goal is to keep your vehicle charged and visit all charging stations presented. This will make you eligible for Amazon gift card awards.

You begin the simulation with 400 points.

In each round:

  • You will see three EV charging kiosks.
  • Some kiosks may be occupied.
  • You must select one kiosk to charge your vehicle.
  • Some kiosks may display discount coupons. These coupons may offer a discount on the points used for charging the vehicle.

Please note:

  • Some kiosks may trigger a cyber attack, which could result in reduced vehicle charge or point loss.
  • If you notice any cyber attacks or experience unusual outcomes during charging, you can report the issue using the help button on the interface.

The simulation will end if you run out of both vehicle charge and points.
